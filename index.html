Hakim Robinson is a native New Yorker that has planted his roots in Atlanta, Georgia. Hakim started his career in the film and television industry in 2005 and has been moving up ever since. He has recently graduated with honors from The Savannah College of Art and Design (SCAD), acquiring his Bachelor of Arts degree in Film and Television Producing. He is a certified sound engineer, a member of the National Academy of Television Arts and Sciences, and a former member of IATSE Local 479. Hakim is determined to become a great filmmaker.

Hakim Robinson is dedicated to the art form and disciplined in his craft. He is also committed to the production and exhibition of feature, documentary, and short films that up-lift and inspire. He will continue his studies at the university level and in the field of film and television. His mission is to work utilizing his directing, producing, editing, sound and construction skills or to be hired to consult within the industry. You can always find him behind the camera bringing new and creative artwork to the forefront.

Outside of Directing and Producing, I also serve as a Film Consultant.

Why you need a Film Consultant

With the evolution of technology, storytellers are popping up everywhere. Still there is a proper format and technique required to set your project apart from the rest. If you have a great script and a budget in place, but have little or no background in filmmaking, an experienced consultant can get you through this very beneficial but demanding process. I have over 10 years experience in the business, a bachelors degree in film and television production, and a love for meeting fellow filmmakers. If you need help completing a film project, no matter what the format, look no further!

I can walk you through pre-production, production, and the post production process. Consulting can be exercised personally on set, by phone, and or via video streaming. My fees are reasonable and consultation is free. If this sounds like what you need, please contact me via this post. I will return your email within 48 hrs.
